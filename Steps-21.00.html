Fuel(P)YOY and Fuel(C)YOY by Continent (Scatter Plot) 21

  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select scatter chart.
  2. Fill up fields values=Continent, Country; X- axis =Fuel(C)YOY; Y-Axis = FUEL (P) YOY; size=Fuel(C)YOY. 
    Tooltips = FuelProd, FuelCons, Fuel(P)YOY, FUEL(C)YOY, CoalProd ,CoalCons, Coal(P), Coal(C), GasProd, GasCons, Gas(P), Gas(C), OilProd, OilCons, Oil(P), Oil(C),
  3. In Format -> Visual:
    X-axis: on, Values: Font=Calibri (12), color=black,
    Title : on, Font=Calibri (12),color=#827301,
    Y-axis: on, Values: Font=Calibri(12),color=black,
    Title : on, Font=Calibri (12),color=#DBA800, 
    Legend: Position- Top left, Font=Calibri (12),color=black,
    Title: on, title text='Fuel (P) YOY',
    Gridlines: off,
    Markers: color: click (fx) and do conditional formatting as below. color conditional formatting: minimum color=#F8E894, middle color=#F3D119, Maximum color=#998303

  1. Category label: on, values: font=Calibri(12), color= black, background=off.
  2. For Format -> General: 
    Properties, Size, Height=305, Width=340, Position, Horizontal= 605, Vertical=415. Title=On, Text=Continent, Font=Calibri(15), Text color= #FFFFFF, Background color=#983800 , Horizontal alignment=Center, 
    Effects: Background=On, Background color= #E6E6E6, Transparency=20%.
    Tooltips: On, Font =Calibri, Size = 10, Label Color = #C8C8C8, Value Color =#FFFFFF.
