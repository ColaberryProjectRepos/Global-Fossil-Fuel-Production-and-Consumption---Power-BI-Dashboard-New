Adding Calendar and Measures Table 10

1. Creating Calendar Table. On Power BI desktop under Modeling click on new table button. Type the Dax formula below in the Dax formula bar, click on the check mark, and save.

Calendar = FILTER(CALENDAR(DATE(2001, 1, 1), DATE(2020, 1, 1)), DAY([Date]) = 31 && MONTH([Date]) = 12)
Year = FORMAT('Calendar'[Date], "yyyy")

2.Creating Measure Table
From the "Home" tab in ribbon, click "Enter Data" and just rename the blank table to "MeasuresForFuel"  and click OK. Click on Measures table in the fields section and then right click and choose 'new measure' and enter 1 measure formula from below and click tick next to it to create that measure. Right click on the measure table to create another measure and repeat the same steps until all the below measures are created in "MeasuresForFuel" table.

Measures:
1. Coal Prod = AVERAGE(Fuel[COAL Production (kwh)])
2. Coal Cons = AVERAGE(Fuel[COAL Consumption (kwh)])
3. Gas Prod = AVERAGE(Fuel[GAS Production(kwh)])
4. Gas Cons = AVERAGE(Fuel[GAS Consumption (kwh)])
5. Oil Prod = AVERAGE(Fuel[OIL Production (kwh)])
6. Oil Cons = AVERAGE(Fuel[OIL Consumption (kwh)])
7. FuelProd = AVERAGE(Fuel[Total FUEL Production ])
8. FuelCons = AVERAGE(Fuel[Total FUEL Consumption])

9. Coal Production YOY = 
VAR CoalProd_CY=AVERAGE('Fuel'[COAL Production (kwh)])
VAR CoalProd_PrevY = CALCULATE(AVERAGE(Fuel[COAL Production (kwh)]),SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR('Calendar'[Date]))
RETURN
IF(ISBLANK(CoalProd_PrevY),0,CALCULATE(CoalProd_CY-CoalProd_PrevY))

10. Coal Consumption YOY = 
VAR CoalCons_CY=AVERAGE('Fuel'[COAL Consumption (kwh)])
VAR CoalCons_PrevY = CALCULATE(AVERAGE(Fuel[COAL Consumption (kwh)]),SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR('Calendar'[Date]))
RETURN
IF(ISBLANK(CoalCons_PrevY),0,CALCULATE(CoalCons_CY-CoalCons_PrevY))

11.Coal(P) =
