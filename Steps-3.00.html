Challenges encountered:

1. Finding a project with good dataset was the most challenging task .

2. In the dataset, the gas, oil production & consumption were in cubic meters and coal production & consumption was in Tons. So, all the data had to be converted to Kilo watt Hour (kwh), to represent the KPIs for total fuel using DAX formulas in the same metrics as Energy.

3. The country and continent tables had to be imported and mapped, to get the continent as a category. While creating the relationship between two tables, all the null values in my continent column had to be cleaned up in the country-continent table, to match the country names in both CC table and fuel table which took a lot of time to research in google and come up with the correct country-continent mapping.
